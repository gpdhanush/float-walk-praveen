## Common Questions

### Q: What happens to my original image?
**A**: The original is not stored. Only the cropped & compressed version is saved.

### Q: Can I crop existing logos?
**A**: Yes! Remove the current logo and re-upload to crop it.

### Q: Why is my image blurry after compression?
**A**: Try uploading a higher resolution original image. The compressor maintains quality but can't add detail.

### Q: Can I undo a crop?
**A**: Click "Cancel" in the crop dialog to discard changes. Once saved to the form, you can click "Remove" and re-upload.

### Q: Does this work on mobile?
**A**: Yes! The crop dialog is fully responsive and works on mobile devices.

### Q: What if I upload a rectangle image?
**A**: No problem! The crop circle will show you exactly what will be saved. Position it to include the important part.

## Troubleshooting

### Image won't upload
- Check file is JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- Ensure file is under 5MB
- Try a different browser

### Crop dialog doesn't open
- Clear browser cache
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
- Check browser console for errors

### Compressed image is too large
- Upload a smaller original image
- Feature targets ~500KB automatically

### Quality is too low
- Upload higher resolution original
- Compression maintains quality but depends on source
